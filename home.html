🍜 Signature Dishes of Linyi
Steamed Fish
Fresh carp from Yimeng Reservoir, steamed with ginger and scallions. Tender, pure, and full of natural sweetness.
Linyi Fried Chicken
Free-range chicken stir-fried with local peppers and secret sauce. Dry, fragrant, and deeply flavorful.
Pancakes
Thin and crispy pancakes made from millet, corn, and sorghum. The taste of Yimeng resilience.
Hotpot
Rich broth made from chicken and beef bones, served with mountain mushrooms and hand-cut lamb.
Home-style Stir-fry
Local black pork with seasonal vegetables, wok-fried with house bean sauce. Simple and comforting.
San Soup
Linyi's soul-warming breakfast soup. Beef bone and wheat kernels simmered for hours, finished with egg and cilantro.
🌟 More Signature Flavors
Eight-Treasure Black Beans
A Linyi specialty made from eight premium ingredients, naturally fermented for over a year.
Yimeng Lamb Soup
Whole lamb simmered until the broth turns milky white. A winter classic that warms you inside out.
Braised Rabbit Head
A legendary Linyi street food. Spicy, numbing, and packed with umami. Unforgettable.
Guota Tofu
Egg-coated tofu pan-fried until golden outside, silky inside. Simple and delicious.
Junan Donkey Meat
"Dragon meat in heaven, donkey meat on earth." Lean, tender, and rich in flavor.
Yishui Fenggao Cake
A traditional sweet cake made from sticky rice, sesame, and osmanthus. Soft, chewy, and sweet.
🎁 Souvenirs · Take a Bite of Linyi Home 🎁
Yimeng Pancake Gift Set
The perfect gift from Linyi — thin, crispy, and made from five grains. This gift set includes multiple flavors like millet, corn, and sorghum. Pair it with local scallions and fermented black beans for an authentic Yimeng breakfast experience.
Linyi Fried Chicken Sauce
The secret behind Linyi's famous fried chicken! This jarred sauce contains a blend of soybeans, chili, and over ten spices. Just stir-fry with chicken pieces, and you can recreate the authentic Linyi flavor in your own kitchen anywhere in the world.
Mengshan Honey
Harvested from the pristine forests of Mount Meng, this pure honey is naturally rich and aromatic. Collected from wild flowers and medicinal herbs, it's a healthy gift that brings the sweetness of Yimeng nature straight to your tea.
Yimeng Chestnuts
Grown in the mountain forests of Yimeng, these chestnuts are naturally sweet and creamy when roasted. Vacuum-packed for freshness, they make an excellent snack or ingredient for braised dishes. A true taste of autumn in the mountains.
Babao Fermented Black Beans
The most iconic Linyi specialty! Made from eight ingredients including soybeans, eggplant, and ginger, then naturally fermented for over a year. This gift box is perfect for food lovers who want to explore authentic Shandong pantry flavors.
Linyi Sesame Cake
A traditional baked flatbread covered with toasted sesame seeds. Crispy on the outside, soft and layered inside. Individually packaged to stay fresh, it's a beloved snack that reminds every Linyi local of their childhood.
🏞️ Besides delicious food, there is also breathtaking scenery 🖌️
Yimeng Mountain
The majestic Yimeng Mountains stretch for 800 li, boasting magnificent natural scenery and earning the reputation of a "natural oxygen bar." Climbing to the summit offers a panoramic view of Shandong Province.
Wang Xizhi's Former Residence
The former residence of the Sage of Calligraphy, located in Lanshan District. Stroll through classical gardens and admire calligraphic stone carvings.
